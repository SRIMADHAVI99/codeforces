Given a number N. Print the maltiplication table of the number from 1 to 12

For example: if N = 1

Input
Only one line containing a number N (1 ≤ N ≤ 50).

Output
Print 12 lines according to the required above.

Examples
Input
1
Output
1 * 1 = 1
1 * 2 = 2
1 * 3 = 3
1 * 4 = 4
1 * 5 = 5
1 * 6 = 6
1 * 7 = 7
1 * 8 = 8
1 * 9 = 9
1 * 10 = 10
1 * 11 = 11
1 * 12 = 12
